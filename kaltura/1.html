Kaltura BrightSpace Extension

The Kaltura Brightspace Extension is a Learning Tool Interoperability (LTI) that is meant to synchronise with the Brightspace by D2L Learning Management System (LMS). Following this LTI the software that is integrated into the LMS will allow users to create, store and add multimedia content into their online courses.

This course will cover the primary tools (Media Gallery and My Media) that will be integrated into the LMS as a direct result of the LTI. Additionally, this course will also cover two uses of the ‘Add Kaltura Media’ function as well as a common use case (Course Rollovers) that the Education Technology Services team will need to familiarise itself in order to serve it’s clients.
