Categories

Categories are labels that identify which tool and where videos are displayed. When someone creates a video in the Learning Hub, one of the requirement fields is to provide at least one category. When that video is published its created category will automatically appear inside this tool as a subcategory under D2L > site > channels. We can then use that category to manually insert any video from the Entries tab in the KMC to that course Media Gallery in the Learning Hub. For us the categories tool will be used to view all of the currently existing categories that are associated with course Media Galleries in the Learning Hub.
