Moderation

The moderation tool is used to approve videos from Media Galleries inside the Learning hub (and later Mediaspace). If someone who is not the Media Gallery manager (usually students) inside of a Learning Hub course uploads a video and it requires approval beforehand, it will appear in a ‘pending’ tab visible to the manager (usually instructor) and it will also end up here. This tool will show us all of the videos inside every course that requires approval before they can been seen it their respective course Media Galleries. In our case we will likely need to use this tool to triage incidents where video uploaded by students have either gone missing after being approved by the instructor or require approval from someone with authorization who is not the Media Gallery manager of a specific course (like a TA or Program Assistant).
