Personal Options

Inside Personal Options you will find spaces that are unique to your account. These options include:

  • My Media
  • My Channels
  • My History
  • Watch Later
  • Logout

My Media

My Media in Mediaspace functions exactly the same as it does on the Learning Hub. The only difference is that you can publish your videos into Mediaspace Galleries or Channels that you have contributor rights into. Please note that in Mediaspace you cannot publish or unpublish videos into and from the Learning Hub (or vice versa). To publish videos into a Learning Hub course, please use Kaltura on the Learning Hub.

My Channels

My Channels will display the collection of Channels you have manager (or owner) rights into. It is a quick way of directly accessing your Channels rather than having to search for them inside All Channels or a Media Gallery they are published into.

My History

My History will display a list of all of the videos that you have previously watched from the most recent to the latest. You can clear your history by click on the Clear History button.

Watch Later

The Watch Later area will display a Playlist with all of the videos that you have marked ‘Add to watch later’. To view these videos click on ‘Watch Now’, to remove these videos click on Edit, click the ‘–‘ icon next to the video and then click Save.

Logout

Click on Logout option to log out of Mediaspace.
