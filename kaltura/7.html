Rolling Over Course Content

Rollovers concerning Kaltura require two steps. The first step involves a normal rollover the second involves rolling over the Media Gallery.

Step 1

Full Course Rollovers

If you are rolling over an entire course into another then you donâ€™t need to do anything different and just need to worry about step 2.

Partial Course Rollovers

If you are doing a partial rollover or just rolling over the Kaltura Content then you will need to go to Edit Course > Copy/Export/Import > Select Individual Items To Copy. Next, you will need to tick Content and External Learning Tool Links boxes and click Continue.

Step 2

After you have performed the normal or partial rollover you will now need to rollover the Media Gallery items. Go to the Media Gallery and then click on the Media Gallery Options button and select Import. A list of all of the course you have instructor access to should appear, tick the box of the desired course and then click Import.
