Search

Click on the search icon to reveal the Mediaspace search bar. Type in your keywords into the search bar and then on your keyboard hit Enter (or Return). The search feature will do a search inside Mediaspace and then pull up any videos, Channels or Galleries whose metadata most closely match the characters you have typed into the search bar.
