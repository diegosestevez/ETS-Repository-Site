Mediaspace Galleries

A Mediaspace Gallery is a space for a department to store their videos. Depending on the requested settings, this Gallery can either be restricted to specific users or opened to the general public. Upon creation, a Mediaspace Gallery will have an assigned owner (manager). This person will be responsible for configuring the Gallery’s viewing restrictions, adding and deleting Gallery members, enabling moderation and permitting comments for published videos.

To obtain a Mediaspace Gallery, a department must make a request to the ETS team specifying the type of Gallery they would like to have created for them. Additionally, they must also provide the A0 number of an individual they would like as their Gallery’s owner (manager). Prospective Gallery owners must attend a Mediaspace Gallery owner’s workshop hosted by an ETS member if it is their first time managing a Mediaspace Gallery.

Below are the types of Media Galleries a department may request (please note that Gallery managers can change this type after it has been created)

Creating and Configuring Galleries (ETS only)

Mediaspace Galleries are set on the Kaltura KMC. To create a gallery ETS staff should log in, go to the Categories tab and then click the Add Category button. Give the category the name the client has requested and set the parent categories to ‘Media > site > galleries’, then click Apply.

When the Gallery has been created go directly into the Entitlements module. What determines a Gallery’s type boils down to three settings.

Content Privacy – Determines who can see content (videos) inside the Gallery

Category Listing – Determines who can see the Gallery on the frontpage of Mediaspace

Content Publish Permissions – Determines whether members of the public can view videos inside the Gallery (Not applicable unless if the above two settings are set to ‘No restrictions’ )

Gallery Roles

Mediaspace Galleries have several of roles that can be assigned to their members by Gallery managers. These roles differ from viewing only rights to full editing rights. These roles are:

  • Member - Anyone with this role can view content inside a gallery (this role is not necessary to apply if your Gallery is not set to Private)
  • Contributor – Anyone with this role will have the ability to view, create and publish content inside a Gallery
  • Moderator – Anyone with this role can view, create and publish content inside a Gallery. The Moderators can also approve or reject content from being published into the Gallery. (Note: Moderate Content in the Gallery Options must be enabled by the Gallery Manager before Moderators can approve/reject content)
  • Manager – Anyone with this role has full administrator rights into the Gallery. Managers can view, create and publish content into the Gallery. Managers can also enable moderation, enable comments, moderate content, change the Gallery type, add or remove Gallery metadata and assign or remove members and their roles. Gallery managers can also delete the Gallery in its entirety (this action is irreversible, deleted galleries cannot be recovered by ETS or Kaltura)
  • Manger (Owner) – The same as the manger role except this role cannot be deleted by other Gallery managers. One person will be assigned this role upon the Gallery’s creation by the ETS team
