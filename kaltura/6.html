Adding Kaltura Media in Activities

To Add Kaltura Media to Quizzes, Assignments, Discussions, etc. click the Insert Stuff button > Kaltura Media. Then select the video from either the Media Gallery, My Media or created Playlist tabs and click </> embed to insert the video into the Activity.
