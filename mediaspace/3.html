Mediaspace Channels

Mediaspace Channels are another type of video storage repository that Kaltura Mediaspace offers. Like Galleries, Channels exist as a space where content can be published and managed by an individual (or individuals) with a manager role. Unlike galleries, Channels are spaces which can be published. Hierarchically speaking, you think of Channels as child repositories of Galleries (Channels that remain unpublished are the exception).

Channel managers can only publish their Channels into Galleries if they have at least a ‘contributor’ role in that Gallery. If a Channel resides within a Gallery then only managers of the Channel can unpublish the Channel from that Gallery. However individuals who own at least one Gallery in Mediaspace will have the ability to create Channels. This ensures that these individuals are the owners of every Channel that they create for their own Galleries.

Just like Galleries, Channels come in different types. In order to view any kind of Channels in Mediaspace, authentication past BCIT’s SSO is required.

Types of Channels

  1. Private channel – Channel and its contents are viewable by anyone with a BCIT A00 account
  2. Restricted channel – Only members of this channel may see this channel, it’s contents and contribute content

Creating Channels

Mediaspace Gallery owners are the only ones that can create Channels.

To create a channel click on the ‘All Channels’ tab in the navigation bar and then click on the ‘+ Create Channel’ button. Give your channel its title, name, description and tags by which to search for and then set your channel privacy level (type). You can then publish the channel to any Mediasapce Galleries you manage (or that is set to public).

Editing Channels

Channel managers can edit their Channel through the ‘Channel Actions’ button.

The Channel Actions button has the following options available:

  • Edit
    • Details
    • Playlists
    • Users
  • Upload thumbnail
  • Reset Thumbnail
  • Analytics
  • Create channel Playlist

Edit

The Edit tool is subdivided into 3 tabs: Details, Playlists & Users

Details

The Details tab will allow the Channel manager to update the Channel metadata that was initially set during the Channel’s creation.

Playlists

The Playlists tab is where the Channel manager can create, edit and delete playlists for the Channel

Users

The Users tab will allow Channel managers to add users who have access into the Channel. Managers can control who can see, create and moderate content based on the role they assign specific users.

Channel Roles

Member - Anyone with this role can see the Channel and view its contents (this role is not necessary for users to view content unless if your channel is set to Private)

Contributor - This role will allow the ability to create and publish content into the Channel

Moderator – Allows the ability to create and publish content as well as approve or reject content that is published into the channel by

Manager – Full Channel administration rights. Can view, create, publish and remove Channel content. Managers can also edit metadata, add, edit or remove Channel members (except Owner) and edit Channel options

Manager (Owner) – Same as Manager. Automatically assigned to a Channels creator.

Upload Thumbnail

Channel Managers can upload a custom thumbnail for a Channel

Reset Thumbnail (only if thumbnail added)

Remove the Channel thumbnail and reset the header to its default state

Analytics

Allows Channel managers to see the analytics for the Channel

Create channel playlist

Allows Channel managers to create playlists from videos in their My Media and any videos that are currently part of the Channel. Created playlists can be only be edited it the Playlist tab under the Edit option of the Channel Actions button

Adding Videos to Channels

Adding content to a Channel can only be done by members with a Contributor role or higher.

Method 1

Adding already created content from My Media

Inside the Channel make sure you are viewing the Media tab. click on the + Add to channel button. Doing so will allow you to view your My Media inside Mediaspace. Here tick the videos you would like to publish to the channel and then click on ‘Publish’.

Method 2

Add new content directly

Inside the Channel click on the + Add to channel button. Click on Add new and select any option (except video quiz). Create your video entry, then under the publishing status option, select ‘published’, then select the Publish in Channel tab and then select the channel you would like the video to appear in.

Method 3

From the navigation tab on the Home page, click on +Add New and select any one of the video creation options (except video quiz) and fill out the metadata after uploading the video. In My Media tick the box next to the video you would like to publish into the Channel. Next, click Actions and select Publish. Select the Published radio button then the Publish in Channel tab, then select the channel you would like the video to appear in and click Save.

Creating Playlists in Channels

Creating Playlists for a Channel can only be done by Channel Managers

Method 1

To create a playlist enter your Channel and click on the ‘Channel Actions’ button. Next, click Edit and then click on the ‘+ Create channel playlist’ option to create your playlist. Give your Playlist a title, description and search tags if you would like, then click ‘Create’. Under Add Items to your playlist Choose the videos you would like to add from either ‘My Media’ or videos that are already published into the Channel (Channel). Next confirm or rearrange the videos under the Playlist section and then click on Go to Channel to finish creating your playlist.

Method 2

To create a playlist enter your Channel and click on the ‘Channel Actions’ button. Next, click Edit and then click on ‘Playlists’ tab. Under this tab you will see of the all existing playlists that have been created for this channel which you can either edit or delete. Click on + Create Channel Playlist button to create a playlist. Give your Playlist a title, description and search tags if you would like, then click ‘Create’. Under Add Items to your playlist Choose the videos you would like to add from either ‘My Media’ or videos that are already published into the Channel (Channel). Next confirm or rearrange the videos under the Playlist section and then click on Go to Channel to finish creating your playlist.
